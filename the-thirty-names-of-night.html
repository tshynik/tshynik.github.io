The Thirty Names of Night

by Zeyn Joukhadar

✮✮☆☆☆

Probably good art, but I didn't enjoy it. The lavish prose and magical realism were just annoying to read, and I closed it with relief. Several overdone parallels and coincidences, but one of the early central ones (main character's graffiti habit) completely fizzles out. Duolingo Arabic was very helpful.
