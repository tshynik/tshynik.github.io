My goal was 36 and I read 103, counting some same-year rereads. The goal was intentionally low: I like to track how much I'm reading because it correlates with my screentime and mental health (more anxiety = more books, unfortunately), but also because I like making my little lists and collecting stats like the fucking spreadsheet nerd that I am.
