Exile in Guyville: How a Punk Rock Redneck Faggot Texan Moved to West Hollywood and Refused to Be Shiny and Happy

by Dave White

no star rating

Not quite what I expected from the description. White used to write a column about living in West Hollywood apparently, and this is a compilation of his cranky/snarky columns.

Ultimately it's inoffensive but not that funny, and it couldn't hold my attention. If this sounds like your kinda thing, let me know---I'm planning to give away the copy I picked up at the used bookstore.
