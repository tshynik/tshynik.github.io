Pride and Prejudice

by Jane Austen, Anna Quindlen

✮✮✮✮☆

I was surprised how much I liked this. I never thought it was my kind of book, but Sam told me to read it, and she was right. I've seen all the screen adaptations-- most of the humor really doesn't translate well to visual, so I'm not sure what the attraction of making a movie out of it is!
