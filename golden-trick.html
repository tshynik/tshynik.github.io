by Iris Jay, Nero O'Reilly

✮✮✮✮✮

Beautiful. Interesting. Hot.

Edit: Actually I'm giving it an extra star because of just how fucking beautiful the art is. Absolutely cannot be overstated.

I also enjoyed the introductory notes about how this is reclaiming the word PORNOGRAPHY, because having a plot and being a work of art does not make it something more classy/euphemistic like "erotica."
