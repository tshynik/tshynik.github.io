Shadow and Bone (Shadow and Bone, #1)

by Leigh Bardugo

I was led to believe this was total fantasy ya trash, and honestly I don't think it is! Or at least, it's quite successful fantasy ya trash, which is more than I can say for a lot of what's out there on shelves. Very readable, limited amount of pointless denial by the heroine (though there could have been less). I was surprised by probably half the twists, which isn't bad.
