Edgewood (Edgewood #1)

by Karen McQuestion

Got this for free a while ago. Absolute raw marshmallow of a book: white and fluffy and bland as fuck, nothing outstandingly good or bad to snag your attention or distaste, and nothing you'll remember 5 minutes after it's done. Sometimes you just want to eat a marshmallow, even if then you wonder why you did it.

His sister is actually his mom, isn't she. :/
