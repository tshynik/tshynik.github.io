Upright Women Wanted (Sand in the Gears, #1)

by Sarah Gailey

✮✮✮☆☆

Fine, but pretty insubstantial. Feels like a medium between [b:River of Teeth|31445891|River of Teeth (River of Teeth, #1)|Sarah Gailey|https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1484234556l/31445891.SY75.jpg|52146842] and [b:Magic for Liars|34594037|Magic for Liars|Sarah Gailey|https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1537942213l/34594037.SY75.jpg|55736656], but more on the River of Teeth side of the scale. Gailey's writing has massively improved over the past few years, and I'm excited to keep reading their books, but this one was just OK.
