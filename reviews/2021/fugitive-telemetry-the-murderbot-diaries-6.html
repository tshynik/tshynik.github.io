Fugitive Telemetry (The Murderbot Diaries, #6)

by Martha Wells

✮✮✮✮✮

Really, really enjoyed 95% of this. The ending was a bit of a downer though. Yet again the Corporation Rim can only be held at bay, not defeated. But then, that sense of scale is one of the things I really enjoy about the Murderbot Diaries - no one person or group of people is going to solve a massive complex problem, just the ones in front of them.

reread Nov 2023: I missed Murderbot!! I am bumping it up from 4/5 to 5/5 because it was amazing and there are no rules. Enjoyed how the different settings are used to explore different aspects of life on the space station.
