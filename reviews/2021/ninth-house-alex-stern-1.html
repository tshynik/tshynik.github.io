Ninth House (Alex Stern, #1)

by Leigh Bardugo, Lauren Fortgang, Michael David Axtell

✮✮✮☆☆

I honestly wasn't loving this til the mystery started to unravel in the last quarter of the book, but it really perked up! It's not, like, subtle, but interesting and with lots of layers.

A more satisfying murder mystery than the book that it reminded me of ([b:The Likeness|5941114|The Likeness (Dublin Murder Squad, #2)|Tana French|https://i.gr-assets.com/images/S/compressed.photo.goodreads.com/books/1538062804l/5941114.SY75.jpg|6504351]), tbh.
