Doctor Who: Unnatural History (Eighth Doctor Adventures, #23)

by Kate Orman, Jonathan Blum

LOVE IT. Love it. They made me care so much about all the Sams; it's heart-wrenching. The hints at addict!Sam's chronology are horribly chilling; I want to read about how that went down and how her version of the Doctor and Fitz coped, but I don't think I could handle it.

I think Blum and Orman might be my favorite 8DA writers so far. I highlighted some idea or phrasing I loved about every five pages.
