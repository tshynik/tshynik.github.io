Hawkeye, Volume 2: Little Hits

by Matt Fraction, David Aja, Javier Pulido

✮✮✮☆☆

Liked this better than vol. 1, but still nothing to write home about. The "Pizza Dog" story was easily the best---really actually clever and interesting and novel---but I'm still a bit confused about what happened during the climax. The appendix with an explanation of the color theory behind one of the stories was fascinating, but not strictly part of the story.

I may be biased on this one, because I don't generally like superhero comics. This one is likeable and maybe that makes it well above par.
