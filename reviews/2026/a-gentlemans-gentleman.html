A Gentleman's Gentleman

by T.J. Alexander

Well, I read that FAST, oops. Under 12 hours from library pickup to finishing past midnight. A delightful snack of a book, struggled to put it down. The short and stout hero who doesn't really mind being short and stout - helllll yeah. Strong personalities. Good shit. I'm really waffling between rounding my 4.5 stars up or down - at the end of the day this is, as I said, a snack.

Minor complaint: why is the cover illustration of someone in Christopher's kind of clothes with a very thin face and dark chin stubble? C'mon.
