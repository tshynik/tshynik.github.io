Winter Tide (The Innsmouth Legacy, #1)

by Ruthanna Emrys

✮✮✮✮✮

4.5 rounded up to 5

Chosen family, religion, the pressures of cultural continuity, a smattering of queer characters. I like how Spector's Jewishness is written, and appreciate the parallel between Winter Tide and the days between Rosh Hashana and Yom Kippur, and of course I'm going to be thinking about that this coming new year!
