Frankissstein: A Love Story

by Jeanette Winterson, John Sackville, Perdita Weeks, Harrison Knights

✮✮☆☆☆

A mess, but not bad enough to arouse passion. Various author mouthpieces philosophizing about intelligence and the body. Almost no plot; the characters are cursory. A very cis attempt to shoehorn transness into it, to complete the current events roundup.

The male reader's accents were dreadful, it would have been much better if he hadn't tried them.
