The Girls' Guide to Hunting and Fishing

by Melissa Bank

This is not my kind of book (I'm not so much into books about people in relationships, especially lit-y ones, because they tend to be about people in unhappy, dysfunctional relationships), so I'm surprised to report that I really enjoyed this and didn't struggle to get through it at all. I'm not going to say I understood it perfectly, but it was interesting and beautifully written and gave me some new insights on what it's like to be an adult. I'd give it 4.5 if I could.
