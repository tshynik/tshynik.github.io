My Volcano

by John Elizabeth Stintzi

Calling it quits at pg 214 (plus flipping through the rest). Far too much body horror and lovingly described natural disasters for my taste. The form is interesting, but I Just Can't with the content.

I can't say I get what the point is either, other than re/creating an atmosphere of apocalyptic anxiety for its own sake.
