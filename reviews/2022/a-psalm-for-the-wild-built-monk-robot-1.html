A Psalm for the Wild-Built (Monk & Robot, #1)

by Becky Chambers

✮✮✮✮✮

I'm not going to say this is the best book I've ever read or extremely high art, but it is very good and I'm rounding up because I really enjoyed it.

I got to the part describing the new ox-bike and said to myself "ohhh, Sam really needs to read this, it's about someone having a cute tiny home and doing introvert things" and once she started it she came and told me how much she wants an ox-bike.
