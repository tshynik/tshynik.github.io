Victories Greater Than Death (Unstoppable, #1)

by Charlie Jane Anders

✮✮☆☆☆

Truly middling; very YA. I found myself rolling my eyes and starting to check out in places, but then I'd keep reading and get sucked back in. Might read the next books---I certainly wanted to find out what happened next when I hit the end---but then again, I might not.

More space-fantasy than sci-fi if I had to choose a category.
