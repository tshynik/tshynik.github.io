by Higu Rose

I think the one I read is a collection of #1-4. Has high aspirations (slice-of-life about people behaving imperfectly; nonlinear storytelling). Doesn't meet them, but I have to give it credit for trying. Even with the author trying to spell it out, I couldn't parse who was warning who about what with respect to the drug dealer dude.
