Jonathan Strange & Mr Norrell

by Susanna Clarke

I'd really err more on the side of 4.5 stars. I read the last 600 pages of the book in about a week, whereas it took me more than a year to get a purchase on the first 200. That was a miscalculation on the part of the author.

There really is a distinct turning point, though, and it really is worth it to get there.
