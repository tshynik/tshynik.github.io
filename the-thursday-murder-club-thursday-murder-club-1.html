The Thursday Murder Club (Thursday Murder Club, #1)

by Richard Osman

✮✮✮✮☆

More of a 3.5, but I'll round up because it does what it says on the tin---it's not aiming to be high art, it's aiming to be a mystery novel, and it delivers. The journal entries/different voices help add some variety to keep it interesting. I also did laugh out loud several times, so.
